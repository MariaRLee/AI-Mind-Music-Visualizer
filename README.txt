Important:
- The built-in dance rhythms are synthesized in the browser.
- They are rhythm demonstrations, not commercial songs or copyrighted recordings.
- Local music files are not uploaded or stored.
- No analytics or external network connection is used.

How to use:
1. Download AI-Mind-Music-Visualizer-v2-standalone.html
2. Double-click it to open in Chrome or Edge
3. Choose local brainwave music, or select a dance rhythm
4. Choose a visual mode
5. Press Play
