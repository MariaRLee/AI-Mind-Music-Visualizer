AI-Mind Music Visualizer V3.1

New visual modes:
- Abstract Geometry: colorful orbs, flowing curves, and geometric shapes
- Others / Custom: users can enter a supported safe theme

Supported custom themes:
- Hearts
- Rainbow
- Bubbles
- Snowflakes
- Music notes
- Flowers
- Ocean
- Fireworks
- Stars
- Butterflies
- Clouds
- Light

Unsupported, unrecognized, or blocked words display:

Important:
This website remains fully local and does not connect to generative AI.
Therefore, custom visuals use a safe, predefined theme library rather than generating any arbitrary requested image.
